Privacy Policy

How VinDetect.ai protects your privacy and data

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

VinDetect.ai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our AI-powered stolen vehicle detection system.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Organization name and professional details
  • IP address and geolocation data
  • Browser information and device identifiers
  • Communication preferences and history

2.2 Technical Information

  • Website usage data and analytics
  • Log files and server data
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Performance and diagnostic information

2.3 VIN and Vehicle Data

For law enforcement and authorized users, we may process Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) and related vehicle data solely for the purpose of fraud detection and stolen vehicle identification. This data is processed in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our AI-powered detection services
  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support
  • Communicating about our services and updates
  • Analyzing usage patterns and improving user experience
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Authorized Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Law enforcement agencies and government entities (when legally required)
  • Trusted service providers and business partners
  • Legal advisors and compliance consultants
  • Third-party vendors for technical services (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or government regulation, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Multi-factor authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance and FISMA-ready infrastructure
  • Employee training on data protection and security protocols

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. VIN and vehicle data processed for law enforcement purposes may be retained according to applicable law enforcement data retention policies.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance website functionality and user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
